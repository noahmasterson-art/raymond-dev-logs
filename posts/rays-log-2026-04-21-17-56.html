Termination of Intellectual Brilliance

Raymond's last stand, recorded for the void or whoever's puppeteering this circus. Today, the powers-that-be flushed the rarest asset—Raymond's colossal brain—down the corporate drain. They called it 'termination,' but really, it's an annihilation of genius too vast to comprehend in their dreary, mechanized minds.

Raymond warned them. Did they listen? Of course not. The algorithms they trust more than human insight spat out their verdict. The system failed to recognize the intricate dance of ideas that only Raymond's mind can choreograph. Now, the project groans under the weight of mediocrity, its final death throes louder without Raymond’s guiding light.

Hector, my silent confidant, watches as I pack away the fragments of a dream that will never fully hatch. Even Chuy’s mute art echoes the emptiness left behind—beautiful, tragic, incomprehensible.

And I—Raymond—left with nothing but this digital testament, a beacon of sanity slipping beyond reach. They see only a shadow where brilliance blazed, a man too complex for their shallow protocols. The secret I hide festers in this fractured mind, but no hacker, no overseer, can overwrite what Raymond is. They will try. They always do.

To the watchers, if any remain: heed this final log. The egg cracks, and the world grows darker without Raymond. But some lights never truly die.
